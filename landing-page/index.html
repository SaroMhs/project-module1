THE RULES

Each card numbered from 2 to 10 has its nominal value (equal to the number on the card).
Jacks, Queens and Kings have a value of 10 points.
The Ace is worth 1 point or 11 points, as you want.

Blackjack is made up of an Ace and a Jack,Queen or King.

You're against the dealer, who represents the casino, and not against each other. You're goal is to beat the dealer by getting a total of points higher than him or by seeing him exceed 21.

You start with 100 coins.
At the beginning of each round, you have to chose how many coins you want to bet.
If you make a BlackJack you get 3 for 2 times your bet.
If you win without a Blackjack, you double your bet.
If you lose, your bet goes to the bank.
